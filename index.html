Bring your bunny

This event will provide an opportunity for those working on fabrication to meet others and share their experiences. We encourage them to bring physical objects (fabrication results) to make it a fun event. We plan to invite people working on fabrication widely using our own connections, but also welcome anybody interested in this topic.
